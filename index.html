About Me

I am currently a second-year Ph.D student in Computer Science at University of Southern California(USC), supervised by Dr.Emilio Ferrara. I am affilicated with Machine Intelligence and Data Science(MINDS) research group at Information Sciences Institute of USC. Prior to USC, I got my master degree at Télécom Paristech in France and my bachelor degree in Zhejiang University in China.

My research focus is on graph embedding, representation learning, computational social science and machine learning. I am interested in analyzing large social networks from graph learning perspective, exploring and understanding graph representation.
